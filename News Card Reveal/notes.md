# News Glass-Card Editorial Reveal

## Primary Use Case

A cinematic glass-card poster reveal built for a principal subject holding a newspaper or occupying a strong front-facing editorial frame, supported by a large background "NEWS" wordmark, red edge accents, and subtle particle energy.

## Best Used For

- editorial news-themed visuals
- media or journalism poster intros
- announcement cards
- cinematic hero panels
- premium issue-cover style reveals
- political or commentary visuals
- glassmorphism character cards
- dark news-branding intros

## Visual Behavior

The composition opens as a premium rounded card with a soft glass shell. Behind the main asset, a large background NEWS title settles in as a structural cue. The principal asset then blur-ups into place inside the frame. Red vertical accent glows at the edges energize the composition, while small red particles and surface shine give the scene a polished cinematic finish.

The motion should feel premium and deliberate, not flashy.

## Motion Sequence

1. The glass card shell establishes first.
2. The background NEWS typography resolves in with blur reduction.
3. The principal asset rises in with blur-up and slight scale correction.
4. Red accent glows come alive at the frame edges.
5. Shine and particles appear to enrich the composition.
6. The frame settles into restrained ambient motion.

## Works Best With

- front-facing seated or standing characters
- newspaper or document props
- red-and-black accent systems

## Avoid Using When

- the design should feel playful or lightweight
- the image lacks a strong central subject
- the composition requires many text blocks
- the tone is soft corporate rather than editorial
- the visual system should be flat and minimal with no atmosphere

## Customizable Elements

- principal asset source
- background wordmark text
- card corner radius
- red accent glow strength
- particle count and position
- shine intensity
- blur intensity
- card inset
- animation speed
- ambient drift amount
- scene dimensions
